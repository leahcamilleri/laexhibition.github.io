Amelie Dimech

Amelie's work focuses on mixed media and the relationship between canvas and media. Her work plays with the idea of what defines an artistic medium, employing unusual materials in her art to push this boundary.

Leah Camilleri

Leah's artistic style is defined by her vibrant use of bright, bold colors and her mastery of acrylic paints, which bring her work to life with energy and intensity. Her creative focus lies in exploring the intricate beauty and expressive forms of the human body, paired with the dynamic compositions of still lifes. Each piece reflects her ability to blend striking color palettes with meticulous detail, creating visually captivating and emotionally resonant works that draw the viewer in.
